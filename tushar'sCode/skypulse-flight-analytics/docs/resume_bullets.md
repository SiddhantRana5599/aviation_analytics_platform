- Implemented Medallion Architecture with Bronze raw ingestion, Silver deduplication and standardization, and Gold business KPI tables.

- Designed Airflow DAGs for scheduled validation, orchestration, and ML retraining workflows.
- Delivered a professional Streamlit dashboard for live route analytics, streaming monitoring, historical trends, and price prediction.
